Knowledge, Skills & Hard Work

Skill, Knowledge, Hard work and Attitude ! Skill, knowledge and hard work are self earned and self determined. Attitude is linked to behavior and behavior changes with the surrounding environment. Employees walk into an organisation with a positive opinion based on earlier discussions with the employer. Means the employee makes an entry with a positive attitude.

This attitude might change over time with the surrounding environment, over commitment made, mismatch in job role, job satisfaction, etc. Therefore it is very important to ensure full clarity and understanding before making a job offer and before accepting the same. There is no definite method to measure Attitude. Attitudes can be difficult to measure because measurement is arbitrary, meaning people have to give attitudes a scale to measure it against, and attitudes are ultimately a hypothetical construct that cannot be observed directly.

Work Hard In Silence

When you talk about your goals, it already gives you that dopamine. It already makes you feel like you achieved the goal even when you didn’t. Even if you already feel like you made it, remain silent about your goals and intent.

The more publicly you announce your next move and what you’re going to do, the less likely you’re going to even start on them. You just added unnecessary extra pressure that you didn’t have to add to yourself. Now, people are going to expect for you to do something.
